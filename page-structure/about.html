Welcome to Radarange - The Groundbreaking Microwave Oven by Raytheon

Our Heritage

Our story is rooted in innovation and an unwavering commitment to quality. It all started when an engineer from our parent company, Raytheon, discovered that microwaves could cook food quickly and efficiently. This groundbreaking discovery led to the creation of the first commercial microwave oven, the Radarange. We were the first to market with this revolutionary product, setting a standard for kitchen appliances worldwide.

Our Mission

At Radarange, we believe in making cooking easier, faster, and more enjoyable for everyone. Our mission is simple: to bring the efficiency and convenience of microwave cooking to every household and professional kitchen. We strive to continually innovate and improve our products to meet the evolving needs of our customers.

Our Products

From the very first Radarange, an imposing and robust machine, to the sleek, user-friendly, and energy-efficient models of today, our product line has evolved immensely. We take pride in offering a diverse range of microwave ovens that cater to different lifestyles and cooking needs. Our products are known for their reliability, durability, and groundbreaking technology.

Our Commitment to Quality

Quality is at the heart of everything we do. Each Radarange microwave is designed with precision, manufactured to the highest standards, and rigorously tested to ensure that it meets our stringent quality criteria. Our dedication to quality is why millions trust us to deliver exceptional cooking experiences.

Looking Ahead

As we move forward, our focus remains on innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction. We are dedicated to continuing our legacy of transforming kitchens and changing the way people cook and enjoy food.
